Entities and Special Characters

Go high or go low

The Superscript element and Subscript element are often used in mathematical and scientific notation. They effectively make a character hover slightly or drop lower.
H2O is an example of Subscript and is a useful tool for writing symbols in chemistry and also keeping you hydrated.
X 2 + Y 2 is an example of superscript and can be used for writing algebra equations in mathematics.
